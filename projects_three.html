Indigenous People of Ciptagelar

On August 22th 2022, I have an opportunity for attending "Seren Taun" an annual ceremony for Ciptagelar Indigenous village. This ceremony is a peak agenda of harvest period in a small indigenous community in Sukabumi Regency, West Java. The ceremony began when the first rice grain harvested by the people was inserted into 'leuit', a traditional granary of Sundanese people led by Abah Ugi, their famous tribe leader
